By Leo

War not needed

Sad reality of today

As simple as a flower,thousand structures that humans can never understand,
Oxygen,molecules,deep waters and the shape of your hand,
As they think they are the heros,the kings and the highest law,
while they don't understand the things they draw.

And what gives you the right to pretend and act like a god,
and what gives you the right to shed another persons blood.
If you cannot rebuild you shall not destroy,
in the hands of a human lives are just a toy.

An noor

The light


The little noor,
a little more to reach the door.
With one more life would it be the same?
Ya rabb you know i'm drown in shame.

Missed again a chance
for you to forgive my sins.

Thousand steps 

And lovien more above

You gave fire strength to burn me,
but it warms me and at night it helps me see.
You let the water drown me,
and yet it saves my life.
Your wind shall blow on your command,could break my lovely home,
but still gives me air so i can breathe. 

You feed me,cloth me my protector,
and still i cry for more.
Oh by life you gave me,
ya rabb forgive me and make me see.

The sky

Beautiful creation

I loved the lights above me since i remember how to breathe,
in darkness of the night they always have been lit.
Little lights,giant lights and lines that never sleep,
little lights that always listen,listen when you weep.

And a queen of night at late hour shines,
such a perfect circle while hit by the fastest lines.
And it goes on forever,day by day for more,
such amazing life,i wonder what it's for.

But i already know, don't I,
I remember times when all i did was cry.
Wasted life to it's fullest,i wonder why,
when i really could do much more,and my mind would fly.

Balad to the lord

The all knower the all hearer

The perfect of all beauty,couldn't be degraded,
along with darkest corners,I can't imagine if it faded.
And never goes away but rather only spreads,
always been too big to fit in our heads..

But you always forget that it's not you the one who is the best,
and you forget that life shall be your test.
And when did it happen? Above you he sees it all,
he sees you when you rise,he sees you when you fall.

Iman

The key


A power that could lead my pen,
it always existed but I never thought of it back then.
I always had my little secret,keeping it from ears,
little,little secret where I hid my fears.

Minute after minute it's keeping me from harm,
but still i can't fight your charm.
A simple red string from me to you,
doors above are open,just a simple step shall do.

But keeps me back a promise,a promise that i made,
to keep this faith in memory,and never let it fade.
I just haven't finished yet and I'm running out of time,
but i put my trust in you and him,I trust that i'll be fine.

Adhan of the night

Fajr adhan


Silent night can wake you up,
the voice of life that burns shaytan that rest in heart.
Repent and cry to fill the gap,
to let your angels keep their guard.

Not to harm but to remind,
prevent your heard from going blind.
Tears from eyes you cannot fight,
the words of lord from highest heights.

A lonely soul is satisfied and ears can no more bleed,
with voice of life you are complete.
Bigger joy than any eid,
and darkest mind is light and lit.

A grown man cries by hearing sound 
repents and falls down on the ground.
